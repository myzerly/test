About Me

My 5 year stint as a chef in the fine dining realm encompasses most of my professional design experience. I have worked in two Michelin starred restaurants, where as a small team of six we went through an iterative design process involving the three major aspects of a meal: plating, service and taste. As a team we created the culinary experience and I want to bring those hard-earned fundamentals that I developed in the kitchen to my future endeavors.
